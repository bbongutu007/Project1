Who Was "The Glide"?

Clyde" The Glide" Drexler was a NBA Basketball player who was a part of the league for 16 years. The 6' 7'' 210 pound Shooting Guard was born June,22,1962 in New Orleans, Louisiana. He attended the University of HoustonOver 16 years in the NBA, the 10x all star averaged 20.4 points a game, 5.6 assists, while shooting 47.2% from the field. He was the 14th Overall pick in the 1983 draft by the Portland Trail Blazers where he would spend 14 seasons, before leaving for the Houston Rockets. Clyde would be inducted into the NBA Hall of Fame in 2004 and rightfully so. Clyde was also a member of the legendary 1992 Olympic Dream Team who would go on to win gold in the Olympics.
