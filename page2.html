    • Who was Larry Legend?

    Larry Bird or Larry "Legend" was a 6' 9'' small forward from West Baden Springs, Indiana. He was born on December 7, 1956 in West Baden Springs, Indiana and would end up staying in Indiana for college attending Indiana State for 4 years. After an illustrious college career ,he was drafted 1st round 6th overall by the Boston Celtics, where he would spend his 13 year career. Over his NBA career he attained his "Legend" status by averaging a double-double over his whole career with 24.3 points per game, 10 rebounds per game and 6.3 assists per game. His ability on the court was not his only gift however. Bird would also become NBA Coach of the Year in 1998 for his coaching job for his hometown team, the Indiana Pacers. Now Larry Bird is an Executive for the Indiana Pacers and still know as one the greatest to ever play the game.
